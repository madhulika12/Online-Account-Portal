Information Collected from Cookies and Other Technologies

When you visit this Platform, your computer may be assigned one or more "cookies.” Cookies are text files containing a small amount of data, usually a unique string of letters and numbers. They are commonly used throughout the Internet and are stored on and accessed from your device when you visit a website (including the Sites).

You can find more information at www.allaboutcookies.org.

Both session ID cookies and persistent cookies are used. Specifically, session-based cookies are used in order to store language and other country specific preferences such as support contact information. Cookies may also be used in order to track internal metrics of site usage. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser.

A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies also enable tracking and targeting the interests of our users to enhance the experience on this site. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s “help” file.

The use of cookies by our partners and tracking utility service providers is not covered by our Privacy Notice. We do not have access or control over these cookies. Our partners and tracking utility service providers may use persistent cookies to help with site optimization.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you wish to block the cookies from the Platform, please visit the “settings” features of your mobile device, or browser and follow the instructions. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the Platform.

Some of your web and email content may also contain small, invisible images sometimes called web bugs or web beacons. When you access the web and emails that contain web beacons, communication is made to a server(s) in order to request the small image. This allows counting of visitors who have viewed these pages or emails. Web beacons collect limited information which may include a cookie number, time and date of the page that was viewed and a page description.

In addition to the above, we may collect limited information from your mobile device in order to provide the App. Such information may include your mobile device type, mobile device ID, and date and times of Mobile Application use. We may deploy tracking technologies within the Mobile Application to help us gather aggregate statistics, but we do not use Personal Information for such purposes.
